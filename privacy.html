Privacy Policy — Daily Prayers Uploader

Last updated: October 2025

1. Overview

The Daily Prayers Uploader app is operated by Daily Prayers Studio and used solely to manage and publish authorized video content on official Daily Prayers TikTok channels. This app does not collect, store, or share any personal data. TikTok access is used only for authorized uploads through the official TikTok Content Posting API.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

  • No personal information such as names or emails is stored.
  • No analytics, tracking, or advertising tools are used.
  • No information is shared with third parties.

3. Authentication and Access

When you connect your TikTok account, the app uses TikTok’s official OAuth2 process to obtain temporary permission to upload videos on your behalf. Tokens are stored securely and may be revoked at any time via TikTok settings.

4. Data Security

All actions occur within TikTok’s encrypted API sessions. The app keeps no personal database beyond technical authentication tokens.
